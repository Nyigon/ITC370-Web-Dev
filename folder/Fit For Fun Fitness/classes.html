Fit for Fun Fitness
Explained/Classes

Below is a list of classes that are offered at Fit for Fun Fitness.
Cross training is a simple concept. It just means exercising in a variety of ways.
Sprints are what they sound like Sprints.
BodyPump is a nice workout.
MixxedFit combines explosive dance movements with bodyweight toning.
Spin is a class where we pedal stationary bikes.
BodyCombat is a boxing and self defense class.
Step Interval is a challenging cardio workout using a step combined with intervals of strength conditioning.
Zumba is a fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance moves.
